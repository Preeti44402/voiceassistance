Welcome! This demo supports both text and voice input.

For voice: Click the microphone button, allow microphone access, then speak.
For text: Type your message and click "Send Text".

Voice Input Instructions:

  1. Click the "Click to Enable Voice" button
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  3. Speak clearly and naturally
  4. Say "hi" to test the voice recognition

Note: Voice recognition works best in Chrome, Edge, or Safari browsers.
