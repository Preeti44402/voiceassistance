Enhanced Voice + Text Demo

Ready for input
Welcome! This enhanced demo supports both text and voice input with expanded conversation topics.

For voice: Click the microphone button, allow microphone access, then speak.
For text: Type your message and click "Send Text".

Try asking about AI, technology, science, jokes, or anything else!

Topics You Can Ask About:

  • Greetings ("hi", "hello", "hey")
  • Well-being ("how are you", "what's up")
  • Capabilities ("what can you do", "help")
  • Technology ("AI", "machine learning", "blockchain")
  • Science ("quantum computing", "neural networks")
  • General knowledge ("inventions", "history")
  • Philosophy ("meaning of life", "ethics")
  • Fun topics ("jokes", "riddles", "fun facts")
  • Entertainment ("movies", "music", "books")
  • Health ("nutrition", "exercise", "wellness")
  • Business ("entrepreneurship", "innovation")
  • Environment ("climate", "sustainability")

Voice Input Instructions:

  1. Click the "Click to Enable Voice" button
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  3. Speak clearly and naturally
  4. Try saying "hi", "how are you", or "what is artificial intelligence"
  5. Ask about any of the topics listed above!

Note: Voice recognition works best in Chrome, Edge, or Safari browsers.
